Bacon and Mushroom Pasta

Description

A simple one-pan pasta dish with bacon, mushrooms and pesto - ready in under 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 400g of penne pasta
  • 250g of button mushrooms
  • 8 rashers of bacon
  • 4 tablespoons of pesto
  • 200ml of creme fraiche
  • A handful of basil leaves

Steps

  1. Cook pasta in boiling water in a large pan. Slice the mushrooms and cut bacon into bite size pieces
  2. Reserve a few drops of the cooking water, drain pasta. Fry bacon and mushrooms until golden.
  3. Tip pasta and water back into pan, stir over heat for one minute.
  4. Take the pan off the heat, stir in pesto and creme fraiche with basil and stir to combine
  5. Sprinkle remaining basil to serve.
